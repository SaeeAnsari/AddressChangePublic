Uncover your human potential



There is no doubt: you are a success

But the subconscious mind is strong and may be keeping you from your life or business goals. Aware Coaching, Inc. was started to help hold brilliant minds like yours accountable for their commitments and reach their full potential.

Founder Nicole Ansari is an expert in human potential and a Certified Life Coach and. In her sessions, she uses a mindfulness approach to navigate you through self-limiting obstacles that may be keeping you from achieving your dreams. Her ability to know people comes from being a corporate talent discoverer with over ten years of professional sales, talent acquisition, and professional speaking engagement experience. She is an active member and volunteer of the Women In Leadership Foundation, and continues to do mindfulness-based cognitive therapy for self-health and growth, and daily meditation.

It is Nicole’s goal to help you meet yours. Contact Aware Coaching, Inc. [link to “Contact” page] to set up free initial consultation.
